# Publications

Peer-reviewed IEEE conference and journal papers authored during my undergraduate research at **Amrita School of Engineering, Amrita Vishwa Vidyapeetham, Chennai** — Department of Electronics and Communication Engineering.

Research spans millimeter-wave antenna design, reflectarray aperture engineering, CubeSat communication systems, and RF/microwave simulation using CST Microwave Studio.

---

## Research Areas

- Millimeter-wave & Ka-band microstrip patch antenna design
- Reflectarray unit cell engineering and phase synthesis
- CubeSat communication systems
- Slot-loading techniques for bandwidth and phase range enhancement
- Full-wave EM simulation (CST Microwave Studio) with Floquet port and periodic boundary modeling
